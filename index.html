GRAND, GREEN FAIRWAYS

DLF GOLF AND COUNTRY CLUB

At the centre of this inspiringly green lifestyle is the DLF Golf and Country Club. It is home to two signature courses – designed by Arnold ‘King’ Palmer and the ‘Black Knight ‘, Gary Player. The Arnold Palmer course, inaugurated in 1999, was the first in the country to offer night golf. The course has received numerous awards over the years and hosted the Johnnie Walker Championship in 2008.

Opened in 2015, The Gary Player Signature Course is an 18-hole, Par-72 course measuring 7654 yards from the Gold tees. The course has two large lakes, big landforms, expansive undulating greens and unique style bunkering. Strategy is the key to successfully negotiating the course, which can play very differently, depending on the tees you play from. The award-winning course has hosted three editions of the Indian Open and five editions of the Women’s Indian Open, both European and Ladies European Tour events.

The club adds immeasurable luxury to the members’ leisure, with facilities including a sports complex, party lawns, spa and sauna facilities, state-of-the-art locker rooms and The Pavilion, which offers a multi-cuisine restaurant, terrace bar and 360° views of the golf courses.

The DLF Golf Academy is the first professional golf school in the country. The Academy, opened in 1999, and over the last two decades has been instrumental in providing golf instruction to members, juniors, corporates and many top amateurs and professionals in the country.

THE SANCTUARY

Winding its way along Golf Drive, connected by stone bridges and vibrant plant life is a beautifully landscaped park aptly christened The Sanctuary.

Spread over 6.8 hectares, The Sanctuary comprises of a walking track that extends over 2 kms, and unique outdoor experiences like The Meadows, The Sculpture Garden, The Woodlands, The Palmerie and The Sacred Grove. Together, they create an unrivalled sanctuary of serenity amidst everyday urban life.

COME HOME TO SAFETY

Measures taken during the pandemic

DELIVERY OF ALL MATERIALS AFTER SANITIZATION

All materials are delivered to the apartments after 2 levels of sanitization by our trained in-house team. Ensuring there is no contamination from outside.

Safety measures taken at the entrance

It is mandatory for all visitors to show the Arogya Setu app, get thermal screening done, sanitize their hands and wear masks.

Continued operations

All the staff required for the basic minimum operation is housed inside the complex to avoid any contamination from outside.

Safety of staff

The staff members are protected by regular medical check-ups and sanitization. Every staff member on duty has been trained and wears PPE kits.

Common area sanitization in the condominium

All common areas are sanitized every 2 hours. Special garbage clearance for masks and gloves are placed at the service side landing of every floor. Sanitizers are placed at all entry touch points.

Daily essential deliveries

We have partnered with grocers like Food Hall, Spencers, pharmacies and other super-marts to cater to our residents’ requirements without having to visit them.

CARE FOR SENIOR CITIZENS

We are taking special care of our senior citizens. Extra caution, all deliveries at home and complimentary assistance on housekeeping service.

FOOD FESTIVALS

The chefs at our in-house restaurant conjure up delightful food festivals and the dishes are home delivered safely. No more mealtime boredom!

SPECIAL LAUNDRY SANITIZER

Fabric sanitizer used for staff uniforms, linen and laundry. Adding another layer of safety to materials in daily use.
